General FAQs

Q: I did not get my reward.
A: Please check if the offer status is "Verified" or "Completed". If it is still pending or under verification, wait for the specified time. Tip: Meanwhile, we suggest you not to uninstall the app and try out other offers.

Q: Why is my offer status under “verification”
A: You should wait for 24 - 48 hours. After completing the offer, advertisers verify the offer compilation. Usually, the verification time is 48 - 72 hours.

Q: Why offer status show as “expired.”
A: Rewards will not be credited if the offer criteria and rules are not met, if the app/offer is uninstalled immediately after offer completion, or if the advertiser is unable to track the offer completion.

Q: What can I do after my offer expires?
A: Once an offer expires, rewards cannot be credited. You can explore other similar active offers in the app and continue earning rewards.

Q: What is the Pending status shown on my offer?
A: Pending status means the advertiser is verifying the offer completion, and this process is usually completed within 24 48 hours. Tip: Meanwhile, we suggest you not to uninstall the app and try out other offers.

A: The gift card email may be delivered to your Spam/Junk folder, so please check there as well. If you still haven’t received it, contact the merchant directly at XOXODAY by emailing cs@xoxoday.com

Q: I withdrew from my account, but I did not get my money.
A: The transfer usually happens instantly, but the bank may take up to 72 hours or more to process the payment from their side, depending on the circumstances.

Q: When will the Rewards be added to my wallet?
A: The rewards will be credited to your account once advertisers verify that you have completed all the steps mentioned in the offer.

Q: What is an Ongoing Offer?
A: An Ongoing Offer is one you have completed partially, i.e., where you have not completed all the steps and are still in completion progress.

Q: When will I get the referral reward?
A: Your friend must enter your referral code during signup. Once they complete three offers, both of you receive rewards, and you earn a bonus worth 50% of what your friend earns from those offers.

A: The transfer usually happens instantly, but the bank may take up to 72 hours or more to process the payment from their side, depending on the circumstances.

A: “Avg Reward Time” shows the estimated time to receive a reward based on other users’ experience. The actual time may vary depending on offer rules and verification. If the reward isn’t credited after 72 hours, you can contact support.
