Offer Status FAQs

OFFER_ONGOING

Your offer is currently ongoing.

Please ensure all required steps are completed.

Rewards are credited only after successful completion and verification.

OFFER_COMPLETED_UNDER_VERIFICATION

You have completed the offer, and your reward is under verification.

Verification may take 24–72 hours depending on the advertiser and tracking delays.

If the reward is not credited after 72 hours, please contact support.

OFFER_COMPLETED_VERIFIED

Your offer has been completed and verified.

The reward will be credited shortly.

If it is still not credited after 72 hours, contact support.

OFFER_EXPIRED

This offer has expired.

Rewards cannot be credited for expired offers.

Please check the offer validity period before participating.

OFFER_UNKNOWN

We are unable to determine the status of this offer.

Please contact support for further assistance.
